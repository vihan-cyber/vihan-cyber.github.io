Academic Journey

Final-year undergraduate at University of Kelaniya, Faculty of Science — BSc in Physics and Electronics. Deep engagement in quantum mechanics, semiconductor physics, embedded systems and computational modeling.

What Drives Me

I thrive at the intersection of theoretical physics, hands-on electronics, and software. Building hardware-software interfaces, sensor networks and physics simulations keeps my curiosity burning.

Research & Vision

Exploring IoT-enabled physics labs, low-cost instrumentation and ML for signal analysis. My goal: technology that democratizes scientific exploration in emerging research contexts.

🔬 Currently working on: Final year research — "Wireless Sensor Network for Real-time Monitoring of Physics Laboratory Parameters" using ESP32, LoRa, and Python analytics.

🎓 Core coursework: Electromagnetic Theory, Digital & Analog Electronics, Quantum Mechanics, Embedded C, Signal Processing, Python for Scientists.
